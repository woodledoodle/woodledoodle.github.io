Robbing Chobo

After so many years of working at your detested company Chobo, you decide to join the dark side and break into the companies massive vault of treasures and take whats' yours. Alas, there is a giant electronic lock securing the vault. Luckily you bought a key unlocker off the black market that will help you bypass the security! All you need to do is copy exactly the sequence the electronic device shoots back. Hit the Start button to proceed!
